Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How many days can I get my document?

Processing varies per document. TOR takes 5-7 days, while COE takes 2-3 days. Please refer to the table above.

Can I cancel my request?

Requests can only be cancelled within 1 hour after submission. Once the status is "Processing", cancellation is no longer allowed.

Is there a rush processing?

Currently, rush processing is only available for Certificate of Enrollment for an additional fee of ₱50.00.

Can I send someone to pick up my document?

Yes, provided they bring an authorization letter and a photocopy of your valid student ID.
